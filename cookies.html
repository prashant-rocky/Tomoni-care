What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help websites remember information about your visit. They improve site functionality and overall user experience.

How we use cookies

  • Necessary: Enable basic site functions such as navigation and forms.
  • Preferences: Remember settings like language and display preferences.
  • Analytics: Help us understand how visitors use our site to improve services.
  • Marketing: Support optional features like social sharing or personalized content (with consent).

Third-party cookies

We may allow third-party services (such as analytics or social platforms) to set cookies on our site. These providers have their own policies regarding cookie usage.

Managing cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner (if available). Disabling non-essential cookies may affect some features of the site.

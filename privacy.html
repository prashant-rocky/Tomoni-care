Introduction

At Tomoni Care, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and any other details you provide through forms or consultation requests.
  • Usage Data: Information automatically collected when you visit our site, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and interaction data.
  • Cookies: Small files used to enhance your website experience. Refer to our Privacy Policy for details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries, schedule consultations, and provide requested services.
  • To improve our website and services based on usage and feedback.
  • To send relevant updates, newsletters, or promotional information (with consent).
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our users.

Sharing of Information

Tomoni Care does not sell or rent personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in operating our website or providing services, strictly under confidentiality agreements.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. While we take every precaution, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

Your Rights

  • You can request access to your personal data held by us.
  • You can request correction or deletion of your data.
  • You can opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time.

Retention of Data

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. Tomoni Care is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites.

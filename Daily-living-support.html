At Tomoni Care, we provide gentle and respectful support with essential activities of daily living, so your loved ones can stay comfortable, confident, and independent at home. What We Offer

  1. Personal Care Assistance: Helping with bathing, grooming, and dressing while ensuring privacy and comfort.
  2. Meal Preparation: Planning and preparing healthy, balanced meals that match personal tastes and dietary needs.
  3. Mobility Support: Offering a steady hand with walking, moving around the home, and transferring safely.
  4. Light Household Chores: Assisting with laundry, dishwashing, and simple cleaning to maintain a safe and tidy space.
  5. Routine Reminders: Gentle support with daily schedules, including meal times and personal routines.

Why It Matters

Daily living activities are the foundation of independence. When seniors receive the right level of support, they can continue to live in their familiar home environment with dignity. Assistance with these essential tasks not only makes life safer and easier but also enhances physical and emotional well-being.
